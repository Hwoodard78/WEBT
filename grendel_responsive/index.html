Story of Grendel Prime

"We are losing this war, you say. You whisper that my Warframes are too genteel. Very well. Away with chivalry. Let us have monsters. The law of club and belly, hunger and smoke. Beasts of cave and glut. I spread for you a cannibal feast. A toast, to the valor of mere appetite! You tremble! But the drool is on your chins. For you birthed him, in all his innocent obscenity. This... is what you ordered. Grendel."-Ballas.

Grendel Information

Grendel Prime was released on October 18th, 2023. It is the primed variant of Grendel, having extra health and energy and a new madurai polarity. With Grendel Prime's release came primed variants of his signature weapons, those being the Zylok Prime and Masseter Prime.

How to unlock Grendel Prime

Much like the original Grendel, Grendel Prime can be unlocked in multiple ways. During October, you could pay into Prime Access to unlock Grendel Prime and his weapons, along with some unique cosmetics. Grendel Prime's parts and blueprint can be unlocked for free by unvaulting certain void relics, those being:

  • Neo S18(Uncommon)
  • Axi G11(Rare)
  • Meso G6 (Rare)
  • Lith G8 (Rare)
  • Meso A5, B8 (Common)
