How to farm for Grendel Prime

The best known way to farm Void relics is to play Void defense missions.The main reason being because you can unvault multiple void relics per defense mission Now there won't always be a defense mission available for certain relics, but if you see any of the following missions avaliable, make sure to play them so you can get Grendel Prime faster.

Lith Relics

For Lith Relics, the best mission to look out for is the Orokin Derelict Defense missions in the Void

Meso Relics

For Meso Relics, I would reccomend the node IO on Jupiter

Axi Relics

For Axi Relics, Play Xini on Eris since it has a greater chance to spawn as an Axi mission

Neo Relics

For Neo Relics, The best mission is Hydron on Sedna.
