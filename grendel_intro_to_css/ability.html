Grendel Prime's Abilities

  • Feast-Swallow enemies whole and store them in Grendel's gut.Hold to vomit out stored enemies, covering them in toxic bile. Augment makes feast consume health instead of energy
  • Nourish-Regenerate health as Grendel absorbs nourishment from enemies in his gut. While he digests, enemies that attack or are attacked by Grendel suffer viral damage and energy sources provide more energy. these buffs extend to party members. Augment makes nourishment clear status effects and gain status immunity for all enemies in Grendel's gut.
  • Pulverize-Grendel curls into a ball. He heals over time as he rolls, knocking over anyone in his path. Jumping slams Grendel into the ground and generates a damaging shockwave. Augment launches Grendel in your aim direction.
  • Regurgitate-Puke out a bile soaked enemy from Grendel's gut, turning the consumed into a toxic projectile. Nearby enemies are slowed and have their armor dissolved by the bile.
